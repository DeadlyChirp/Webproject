What is FOOD ADDICT?

Founded by CHRISTOPHE AUREGLIA & TANG THANH LONG in 2022. FOOD ADDICT is a popular online directory for discovering local businesses ranging from bars, restaurants, and cafes.

How Does Food Addict Work?

You can search Food Addict via its website on smart devices. Listings are sorted by business type and results are filtered by geographical location, price range, and unique features like outdoor seating, delivery service, or the ability to accept reservations. Food Addict has a strong social aspect and encourages its users to leave written reviews, star ratings, and photos of their experience with each business they visit. Reviews posted on Food Addict can also be reviewed by other users, while popular reviewers have the potential to be promoted to Food Addict Elite status.

How To Add A Business On Food Addict?
  1. Open the Food Addict and create your professional account.
  2. Click on sign-up as professionnal.
  3. Fill in as much information on the business as you can.

    Name, address, and business category are compulsory but taking the time to add the business' hours, phone number, website address, and any other information you have can improve your submission's chances of being accepted.

  4. Click Sign-Up and You are good to go!
